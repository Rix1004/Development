A focus on Will Hunting

Will Hunting is a brilliant yet troubled young man from South Boston with a natural gift for mathematics and an eidetic memory.
Despite his extraordinary intelligence, Will works as a janitor at MIT and struggles with his past trauma and emotional vulnerability.
His life takes a transformative turn when he solves a complex math problem left by a professor, attracting the attention of renowned mathematician Gerald Lambeau.
Through court-mandated therapy sessions with psychologist Sean Maguire, Will confronts his fears, builds meaningful relationships,
and begins to unlock his true potential, all while grappling with the complexities of his identity and future.
